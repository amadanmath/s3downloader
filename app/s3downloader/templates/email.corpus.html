How to download

The links to the files of {{corpus.name}} are in the attachment. The attached links will remain valid for at least 5, at most 7 days. We suggest using aria2 to download them, using a command like this:

aria2c --input-file={{corpus.id}}.lst --max-concurrent-downloads=1 --connect-timeout=60 --max-connection-per-server=16 --split=16 --min-split-size=1M --human-readable=true --download-result=full --file-allocation=none

Feel free to modify the command to suit your circumstances.
